1. Department of Mathematics and Computer Science

Welcome to the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science at the University of Central Missouri. As you explore the information about our programs, curriculum, faculty and student organizations, you'll discover a strong, diverse, and student-oriented academic unit. The faculty are highly qualified and dedicated. Most hold a terminal degree in their field, and are active in their professions either through research and/or continuing professional development. We provide an unmatched education to students in the region, and we're proud of it.
